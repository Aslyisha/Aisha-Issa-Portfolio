About

With a foundation as a General Ledger Accountant and a pivot into Finance Operations, I've spent the last 3+ years ensuring that the "engine room" of finance runs at peak performance. Having worked across both SaaS and service sectors, I understand the unique challenges of managing remote financial systems and multi-currency reconciliations.

I specialize in designing financial systems for scaling SaaS and service-based companies. My approach combines operational excellence (99.5% accuracy across diverse clients) with systems thinking. I've architected 3-day month-end close processes, built IFRS 15 & GAAP compliant revenue recognition models, and designed billing-to-cash workflows that surface exceptions in real-time. My mission: automate the routine, flag anomalies early, and let humans focus on strategy.

I'm driven by the challenge of taking messy data and turning it into a structured, scalable financial roadmap that supports long-term business growth.
